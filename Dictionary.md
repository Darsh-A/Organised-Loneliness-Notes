
# Somatically
actions, experiences, or conditions relating directly to the body, as opposed to the mind, spirit, or reproductive cells

# Impoverish
to make somebody/something poor or lower in quality

# Devitalization
in a philosophical context, refers to the removal of life, vitality, or essential properties from a concept, object, or system, often as part of an analysis or a critical, reducing process. It is frequently discussed in terms of the reduction of complex, living realities into static, manageable, or "dead" components

# Salient
most important or noticeable

# Praecox
Praecox is a Latin term meaning "very early," "premature," or "prematurely ripe"
